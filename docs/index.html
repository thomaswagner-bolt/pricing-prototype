Fuel and parking

By default and for all rentals below 100 km, fuel and parking is included in the price. You can refuel for free at any partner station via the Bolt app and park for free in allowed areas where Bolt Drive operates.

For long distance drives (100+ km), you can exclude fuel and parking from the price for more flexibility. Then you must cover these costs yourself and cannot use the Bolt app/fuel card. You need to return the car with the same fuel level (shown in the app).
